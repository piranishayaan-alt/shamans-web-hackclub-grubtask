What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that focuses on building systems capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem solving, perception and language understanding.

Types of Artificial Intelligence

Narrow AI

Designed to perform a specific task such as voice assistants, recommendation systems, and chatbots.

General AI

A theoretical AI that can perform any intellectual task a human can do.

Super AI

A future concept where machines surpass human intelligence in all areas.

Applications of AI

Healthcare

AI helps doctors detect diseases early and analyze medical data.

Transportation

Self-driving cars and smart traffic systems improve safety and efficiency.

Education

AI tools personalize learning and help students understand concepts better.

FAQ

Is AI dangerous?

AI can be safe when used responsibly and with proper regulations.

Can AI replace jobs?

AI may automate some tasks but also creates new opportunities and jobs.

Where is AI used today?

AI is used in smartphones, online services, healthcare, finance and many industries.
